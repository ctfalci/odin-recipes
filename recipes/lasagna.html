Odin's Chicken Creamy Lasagna

Welcome! The today's dish is a Chicken Creamy Lasagna, my favorite dish, EVER!!!

Pick your apron, and prepare your shopping list to the following igredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato Paste
  • Pasta Sauce
  • 700g of Chicken Breast
  • 500g of Ham
  • 500g of cheese
  • Corn
  • Peas minted
  • Oreagano
  • and Creamy Cheese Maggi

Preparation:

  1. Put the chicken with salt to cook in the presure cooker for 40 minutes
  2. Add tomato sauce on it, and Maggi
  3. Add some water, if the sauce isnÂ´t thick and creamy add some tomato paste
  4. Add the Corn and the minted Peas
  5. Pour the lasagna sheets in the tray, then the chicken creamy sauce.
  6. cover with grated cheese
  7. then add the ham
  8. repeated until you reach the top
  9. then on the last layer add cheese and sauce on the top.
  10. to the effectiviness of the dish, it must be super saucy / juicy, otherwise gets dry.
  11. put in the oven @185C (fan forced) for 75min, otherwise 200C for 60 min
