The "End" Burger

Welcome! The today's dish is The "End" Burger, WOW, just STELLAR

Pick your apron, and prepare your shopping list to the following igredients

  • Hamburger buns cut open
  • 8xLong Streaky Bacon Stripes
  • Sesame
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mustard
  • 600 grams of minced beef or mixed minced meat
  • 4 eggs
  • 8 slices of cheddar cheese
  • pickles a few leaves of lettuce, tomatoes in slices

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees.
  2. Add the bacon for about 15 minutes or until it starts to curl and become crispy.
  3. Divide the minced meat into balls of 150 grams and shape them into hamburgers.
    Make sure the hamburger is about 1 - 1.5 cm thick. Season well with salt and pepper.
  4. Place a frying pan over a high heat. Melt a knob of butter in this. Fry the burgers on both sides until they are well done.
  5. Keep the burgers warm by putting a lid on the pan.
  6. Break 4 eggs into a large pan so that you get four fried eggs. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. When the bacon is almost ready, place the hamburger buns in the oven with the flat side up. Let them brown slightly and then take them out.
  8. Assemble the burger: put a layer of cocktail sauce on the bottom half of the buns.
    Top with a lettuce leaf, 2 tomato slices, a slice of cheddar cheese and then the burger.
  9. Place another slice of cheddar on the still warm burger.
  10. Then top with 2-3 bacon strips and a fried egg. Finish with a few pickle slices and season again with pepper.
  11. Cover with the top half of the rolls, ready to serve! Please, don't forget your sauces.
